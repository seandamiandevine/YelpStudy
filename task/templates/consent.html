Informed Consent

Study title:Restaurant rating study

STUDY PURPOSE

The purpose of this study is to better understand how we make decisions about stimuli we see. In this experiment, you will see a variety of Yelp-style webpages, with restaurants, their average rating, and their price range.

Foreseeable risks or discomforts

The risks and discomforts associated with this study are minimal.

CONFIDENTIALITY

All information related to personal identity will remain confidential and all answers will be stored on a protected computer server. Once a participant has been compensated, their mTurk account number will be stripped from the dataset. At this point, the researcher will have no way to link an mTurk account number to the participant identity. Withdrawal from the study will not be possible after this point. Results will be disseminated at conferences and journals only as group ratings.

COMPENSATION

If you choose to participate in the study, it will take about 60-80 minutes to complete, you will be compensated $4.00 through your MTurk account.

**Your participation in this study is completely voluntary.**

You can refuse to make a response, at any time, can quit the study without any penalty.
